ℹ️ About Generate Video
Turn a still reference (sprite or photo) into a short animated clip using online video models — ideal for idle loops, talking motions, and simple character movement before you refine the loop in Video Prep.
Why Gemini is preferred: Gemini Omni Flash is the recommended default here — it supports image-to-video and true start+end keyframe mode, and the stock animation prompt is tuned for it. You can still switch to Grok Imagine Video (API key or SuperGrok) or Local ComfyUI when that fits your setup better.
Editing the prompt: Replace (Character description) with a simple note about the character and their mood, and (Animation Type) with a minimal description of the actions they perform in the loop. Keep the camera-lock constraints unless you intentionally want camera motion.
